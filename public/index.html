Questions people ask

Is the photobooth free?

Yes! Create a room, share the code, and shoot together for free. No account needed.

Do we need to install an app?

No. It runs right in your browser on phone or laptop. Just allow camera access and you're in.

How does the sync work?

One person creates a room and shares a code. A shared countdown fires on both screens at the same second, so each frame captures both of you.

What themes are available?

Classic, Vintage, Meme, Laundry, Prison, Subway, Airplane, and Karaoke — 8 vibes to choose from.
